Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AgriFarma

General Information

AgriFarma is a comprehensive digital platform designed specifically for farmers in Sindh, Pakistan. It provides access to agricultural resources, marketplace for buying and selling products, expert consultancy services, community forums, and educational content.

Click on the "Register" button in the top navigation. Fill in your details including name, email, mobile number, and choose your role (Farmer, Consultant, or Vendor). After registration, you'll receive a confirmation and can start using all features.

Marketplace

Navigate to the Marketplace section and click "Add Product". Fill in product details, upload photos, set your price, and publish your listing. Buyers can then contact you directly through the platform.

Currently, listing products on AgriFarma Marketplace is completely free for all registered users. We want to help farmers grow their business without additional costs.

Consultancy Services

Visit the Consultancy section to browse verified agricultural consultants. You can filter by specialization, location, and ratings. View their profiles, qualifications, and fees before booking a consultation.

Yes! If you have expertise in agriculture, register as a consultant through your profile. Complete your consultant profile with qualifications, specializations, and set your consultation fees. After verification, you'll be listed in our consultant directory.

Discussion Forum

The Discussion Forum is a community space where farmers can ask questions, share experiences, and help each other. Create a new thread with your question or topic, and other members can reply with answers and advice.

Technical Support

Click on "Login" and then select "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

You can reach us through the Contact Us page. Fill in the contact form with your query, and our support team will respond within 24-48 hours. For urgent issues, you can also find our phone number and email on the Contact page.
